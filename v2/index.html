Cantonese | 廣東話

A Unique, Expressive, and Beautiful Language

Cantonese is an expressive language used commonly in chinese communities in Hong Kong, Southeastern China, Australia, Canada, and the United States. Written and spoken Cantonese has evolved over the last 2,000 years. Today it is becoming easier to use Cantonese with our devices.

Write, Listen, Speak, and Command in Cantonese



Hand Writing

Writing Traditional Chinese through Cangjie, Strokes, or Handwriting is well supported.



Type

Cantonese Romanizations like Jyutping and Yale make it easier to type Cantonese by how it sounds.



Listen

Cantonese text can be read out loud by our phones and computers. Very helpful for learners and those on the go.



Dictate

Cantonese can now be dictated to our phones and computers, saving us from writing or typing out chinese characters. Helpful if you have your hands full.



Voice Assistant

Voice Assistants allow us to set alarms, send messages, make calls, and find information with just our voice.
